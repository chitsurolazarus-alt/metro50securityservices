GENERAL OVERVIEW

METRO 50 SECURITY SERVICE is committed to providing exceptional services by delivering personalized, high-quality and cost-efficient solutions to meet the needs of our clients.

METRO 50 SECURITY SERVICE is a security company providing comprehensive and tailored protective services, logistics, procurement and training for the defense, energy, and financial sectors, as well as high net-worth clients, diplomatic missions and national and international organizations operating in high-risk, volatile, hostile, and complex environments.

We currently have operations and are deployed in challenging environments including Limpopo – Thulamela, Vhembe, Sekhukhune District and Gauteng but also in the Pretoria areas. Our field experience acquired since 2018, has positioned our company within the security industry landscape as a professional and reliable provider.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Metro 50 security services is a black owned and directed by dedicated business minded people. Metro 50 security services is a business established to render security services such as access control, guarding premises and properties, VIP protection, patrolling, searching, and protecting employees/employers/learners/and general public.

Metro 50 security services is having the physical and financial capacity to execute any work effectively. We aim to adhere to job specification and terms of references when dealing with customers.

Metro 50 security services is well equipped with knowing their job and capable of doing the job according to its object, experienced staff, delivery vehicles, finance and good managers. The company is having the capacity to deploy security officers even on twenty-four states of emergency. We use combat security uniform, legal firearms, PSIRA registered security officers, firearm competent security officers and SABS and SANS approved resources, communications radios and cell phones, hand cuffs and handheld metal detectors for security services.

PURPOSE

To be a leader in the security industry by providing enhanced services, relationship, and profitability.

VISION

It is our vision to encourage self-employment amongst women and maintain local economic growth and family stability.

MISSION

The mission of Metro 50 security services is to secure growth, employ local youth and women and contribute to our provinces endeavor to reduce unemployment, poverty and hunger amongst our people and at the same time contributing towards equitable economic growth in South Africa. To be committed to offer reliable cost-effective customer orientated security services by selecting best security guards, train and retain them to deliver quality service to our customers.
